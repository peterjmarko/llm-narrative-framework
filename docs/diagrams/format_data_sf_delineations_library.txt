# The raw, esoteric text library exported from Solar Fire using its 'decompile' feature.
# Format: A plain text file with a custom structure.

# Lines beginning with ';' are comments and are ignored by the parser.
; SUMMARY OF ITEMS INCLUDED
; ...

# A section begins with a line starting with an asterisk '*' followed by the section title.
*Title
Standard Natal Interpretations

# Each entry within a section is also marked with an asterisk, followed by the specific key.
*Quadrant 1 Strong
You see yourself as an independent individual...

# The text for each entry follows on the subsequent lines.
# Multi-line text is common.
*Sun in Gemini
Your Sun is in the zodiac sign of Gemini indicating that you're curious, lively...
|writing, study, numerous telephone calls or even travelling to...
|

# A vertical bar '|' at the beginning of a line indicates a continuation of the previous line's text.
# The parser script (`neutralize_delineations.py`) handles joining these lines.
